Pawly is not just a memecoin; it’s a movement. Dedicated to supporting animal rescues and foundations like IRA, Pawly brings purpose to the blockchain.

Tokenomics

Learn about the supply distribution and how Pawly contributes to its mission:

How to Buy

Buying Pawly is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Download a crypto wallet (e.g., MetaMask).
  2. Add the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network.
  3. Swap BNB for Pawly on a decentralized exchange like PancakeSwap.
